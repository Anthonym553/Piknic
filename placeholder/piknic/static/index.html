Welcome to Piknic

Piknic is a web application developed to help users obtain suggestions for locations to dine at within a local perimeter. A quick and easy to use app for those who do not find enough time in their daily routines to waste searching endlessly for their next meal. Simply click on the "View Map" button to get started. Location will automatically be updated, but if not the user may input own information within the top left to locate the correct area. Click on map to bring up suggestions after obtaining correct location.
